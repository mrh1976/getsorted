Frequently asked questions

What does a car buying concierge do?

A car buying concierge researches fair market pricing, reviews or negotiates your deal, and communicates with dealerships on your behalf. GetSorted is a consulting service, not a dealer, lender, or broker: you make every final decision and sign all paperwork directly with the dealership.

Is a car buying concierge worth it?

For most buyers, yes, if the savings found exceed the flat fee. Our Deal Review comes with a guarantee: if we don't find at least $199 in savings, you don't pay. A well-negotiated deal typically saves well more than the flat fee charged.

How much does a car buying service cost in Chicago?

GetSorted charges a flat fee with no percentage of savings and no referral fees from dealers: $99 for a 30-minute consult, $199 for a full deal review, or $899 for complete negotiation from research through signing.

Is GetSorted a car broker or dealership?

No. GetSorted does not sell vehicles, extend credit, or hold title to any car. We are a consulting service that researches and advises on your deal; the purchase or lease contract is always between you and the dealership or lender directly.

Does a car buying concierge work with leases too?

Yes. We work on new car leases, new car purchases, used vehicles, trade-in strategy, and lease-end decisions.
