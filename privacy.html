Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

GetSorted ("GetSorted," "we," "us," or "our") provides a car buying consultation and negotiation service to consumers in the Chicago area and beyond. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and details about the vehicle or deal you want help with, when you book a call, submit a form, or email us. If you download our free guide, we collect your email address to send it to you.

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages viewed, through standard analytics tools (including Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking).

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the consultation, deal review, or negotiation services you request.
  • To send the resources you request, such as our free guide.
  • To communicate with you about your service, including scheduling and follow-up.
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

How we share your information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except: with service providers who help us operate our business (such as email, scheduling, and analytics providers) under obligations to protect your data; if required by law, subpoena, or legal process; or with your consent.

When we work on your behalf during a deal review or negotiation, we may communicate with a dealership or lender on your behalf, but only with your knowledge and direction.

Cookies and tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies (including Google Analytics and Google Ads) to understand site traffic and measure the effectiveness of our marketing. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not work as intended.

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide our services and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us directly.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

Contact us
